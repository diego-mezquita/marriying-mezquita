• Bunsen

    Our favorite date night location in Dublin! Pop in and put your name on the wait list and then go and grab a drink at one of the surrounding pubs. These burgers (and the sweet potato fries) are totally worth the wait.

  • 3FE - Third Floor Expresso

    By far our favorite breakfast joint in Dublin. It may be a bit out of the way, but put this place at the top of your list for the most delicious “Green eggs and ham”.

  • Leo Burdock

    If you’re looking for a place to try fish and chips, this one comes highly recommended by Diego. Stop by on your way through Temple Bar.

  • Queen of Tarts

    This place is scone heaven and decorated as cute as can be. Stop in for breakfast or grab some sweet treats to go.

  • O’Neills

    This pub is in the heart of the city. Make your way upstairs and grab a table in the room with the big fireplace to catch live music any night of the week.

  • The Brazen Head

    On a sunny day, head over to the Brazen Head, Dublin’s oldest pub. Their outdoor patio space is the perfect place to enjoy the summer weather with a pint of Guinness. If you’re lucky you might even catch one of their Irish storytelling nights.

  • Darkey Kelly’s

    You know you’ve found a good pub when it is full of locals, right? We absolutely loved the music here and the Guinness stew wasn’t bad either.

  • The Porterhouse

    If you love craft beer, this is the spot to go. Order a paddle and choose three options to taste test. We recommend the Plain Porter.
