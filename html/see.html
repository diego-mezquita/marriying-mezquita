• Phoenix Park

    Phoenix park is one of our favorite places to spend the day. Rent a bike just outside the main gate (you’ll need to rent it for at least 3 hours to see the park) and bring a sandwich for a picnic. There is a lot to do and see in this park, including hanging out with the hundreds of wild deer that reside there.

  • The Guinness Storehouse

    This place has been on our wishlist for awhile now, so join us there on Saturday following the wedding for a tour of the factory and a drink in the Gravity Bar.

  • Dublin City Center

    Despite the weather, Dublin always has something going on, from live music to great shopping. Make sure not to miss Trinity College, St. Stephen's Green, Temple Bar & Grafton Street.

  • The West

    If you’re staying in Ireland for a few extra days don’t miss out on visiting the west coast. We highly recommend visiting Galway and the Cliffs of Moher.

  • The Giant’s Causeway

    Take a day trip up to the Giant’s Causeway and Belfast for an unforgettable experience. There are tons of tours leaving daily from Dublin city.
